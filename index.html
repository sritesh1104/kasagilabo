What is Domain Rating (DR)?

DR is a 0–100 score by Ahrefs that measures a website's backlink authority — the higher the number, the more SEO weight a link from that site carries for AnimeOshi. Prioritise accordingly: 50+ is strong, 30–50 is mid-tier, below 30 is lower priority unless audience fit is high.
